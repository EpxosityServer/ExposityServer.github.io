I'm an administrator and the developer of the Exposity server. I'm talking to you today because I want to show you what's exactly Exposity. In a second time, you can get more visibility if you pay an ad. What do I mean by pay ad ? Well, if you pay us an ad, we will publish your server on a specific channel that everyone can see on Exposity. But there is some changes according to the price. By example, if you want an @everyone mention, you will have to pay a little bit more that if you don't need this mention. The highest promotion is a DM all. But on more time, what I mean by DM all ? Well, the bot will send a DM with your ad to all members of all guilds where he is.

You can get the members count who will receive your ads by mentionning the bot.
